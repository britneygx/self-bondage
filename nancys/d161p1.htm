This is the first self bondage tie I am illustrating here. You are introduced to the basic technique and how to use the tools in the tools section. As always, please take every precaution and practice it many times.

You should use slip knot with quick release mechanism and always ensure that the quick release rope end is within the reach of at least one of your hands. You may want to put your hands in the front of body first to get familar with these steps. You may notice that these steps are normally done in the same sequence as a master would tie you up, with exception of using corresponding tools to assist.

To get free, use quick release mechanism of the slip knot on the wrist.
